Use-Case's that our platform serves

Financial Institutions:

Banks and financial institutions can use Precise Extract to extract transaction data from scanned documents such as bank statements, invoices, and receipts, facilitating financial analysis.

Healthcare Providers:

Healthcare organizations can utilize Precise Extract to extract patient information from medical records and insurance documents, streamlining patient management processes and improving data accuracy.

Legal Firms:

Legal firms can employ Precise Extract to extract relevant information from legal documents, contracts, and court records, enabling quicker access to critical information for case preparation and legal research.

Retail Chains:

Retail chains can use Precise Extract to extract sales data from invoices and receipts, helping them analyze sales trends, manage inventory, and make informed business decisions.

Insurance Companies:

Insurance companies can leverage Precise Extract to extract policy details and claims data from insurance documents, enhancing claims processing efficiency and improving customer service.

Government Agencies:

Government agencies can utilize Precise Extract to extract data from various documents such as tax forms, applications, and reports, facilitating data analysis and decision-making processes.

Educational Institutions:

Educational institutions can use Precise Extract to extract student information from enrollment forms, academic records, simplifying student data management.

Supply Chain Management:

Companies can use Precise Extract to extract data from shipping documents, invoices, and purchase orders, improving inventory management.

Human Resources Departments:

HR departments can leverage Precise Extract to extract employee information from resumes, application forms, enhancing HR processes such as recruitment.
