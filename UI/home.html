How It Works?

STEP 1

Upload your file

Files are safely uploaded over an encrypted connection. Files stay secure. After processing, they are permanently deleted. Click 'Upload' and select files from your local computer. Dragging and dropping files to the page also works.

STEP 2

Edit & Review

Once the document is processed, the software would take you to the review screen. In the review screen, you can see the extracted table under line-items. If you find an issue with the extracted data, you can correct and fix it right there.

STEP 3

NLP on Database

Our cutting-edge Natural Language Processing feature analyzes the uploaded data and extract valuable insights, enabling efficient querying and retrieval of information, empowering you with actionable intelligence derived from your documents.

STEP 4

Export your file

Sit back and watch as Precise Extract swiftly extracts the data you need. Export the extracted data in Excel/xls format effortlessly. Say goodbye to headache-inducing manual data entry and hello to streamlined efficiency!

FAQ's

Why do I need to extract tables from a PDF document?

Tables are cleaner data format, and often you need only the data from tables embedded in a pdf document. That's why, businesses find it useful to extract tables from pdf documents and process the data.

Can I convert my document without installing the software?

Precise Extract's free online OCR tool is capable of processing any document online with complete accuracy. So, there is no need to install the tool on your system.

Do I need to train this free table extractor from pdf tool?

The efficiency of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning technology improves with number of sample documents processed. So, it is highly recommended to have at least couple of sample documents ready for training..

Is my data secure and will the information remain protected?

Precise Extract doesn't sell or share your data to any third-party person or organization. Your data is completely secure and confidential, it is highly recommended to have at least couple of sample documents ready for training...
